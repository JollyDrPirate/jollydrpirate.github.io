# Session Summary

017_20230316 The PCs don’t want to show up empty handed to the dinner party, so after a bit of discussion they decide to buy a barrel of ale to give to the hosts. With that done, they set off for the Sandyfoot home

When they arrive, Mark greets them at the door, as well as one of the house servants, James. Somewhat surprisingly, James easily takes the barrel of ale which Ducky had been carrying, and walks it into the kitchen, while Mark leads the PCs into the parlor to meet the other guests.
The parlor is a cozy room with lots of seating, a warm fire burning in the fireplace, and candles throughout the room lending the space a warm, inviting glow. Off to one side a table of hors d’oeuvres and an open punch bowl. Between information from Mark, and overheard conversations as the group moves toward the table, they learn more about the other guests.
Meredin Sandyfoot, the current Sandyfoot patriarch, is in attendance. His sister Mildred Sandyfoot is sitting nearby conversing with him. Meredin is flanked by two tall human men, James and another very similar looking man, Jonathan.
Professor Geronimo Heinz is an academic looking half orc man, wearing a tweed jacket with elbow patches and large glasses. He stands conversing with Madame Latrocia Mactis Thorn, who seems entirely disinterested in the topic, but the Professor is entirely unaware that his audience is not listening.
The PCs move to get drinks, and then to join the conversation with Professor Heinz and Madame Thorn. Alistair maneuvers the conversation such that the professor and Ducky move to their own separate conversation, allowing him to speak on his own with Madame Thorn. Thankful for being freed from the professor, she happily engages in the conversation and eventually agrees to introduce Alistair to Meredin Sandyfoot.
While he is conversing, Alistair is not drinking, but the other two are. As they begin to drink, they begin to act strangely. At one point Grookius blurts out that he killed his entire family, and without any preamble and harely any prompting Ducky professes that they are all questing to find the The World of the Pieces of Fate. Alistair eventually sips his drink while conversing with Meredin Sandyfoot, and realizes that the punch is magically spiked. It seems to be affecting him differently than the others though - while they seem to be compelled to blurt out their thoughts, Alistair is still in control of when he speaks, but may not speak anything but the truth.
Moving to dinner, Alistair has gone quiet in order to not blurt out more secrets, while Ducky and Grookius try and control their unruly minds. Somehow, they are able to make it through dinner (a frankly somewhat dry roasted chicken breast) without any major incidents. As the other guests move back to the parlor to continue the evening, Meredin Sandyfoot holds them back to discuss business.
With all of them, including Meredin, affected by the truth serum in some way, the conversation moves quickly. The PCs learn that Ozamata has taken control of a gang known as The Red Masks, who previously operated separately from his gang, and often fought with The Jugglers. Now, they still fight with The Jugglers, but as pawns in Ozamata’s schemes. The Red Masks’s base of operations is south of the market in midtown. Meredin advises that Ozamata would stop at that location fairly often, as well as the docks. Finally, Meredin offers the help of his bodyguards, James and Jonathan, who are in fact not human but centaur twins who only inherited the human halfs of each of their parents. Meredin gives Alistair a one time use sending stone, which will call those two to it if it is smashed. Meredin also gives each of them a golden ticket to the show at The Guildhouse tomorrow evening, which will mark them as under his protection to all members of The Jugglers.
With their business concluded, they all return to the party. With the ale flowing, each of the pcs takes a chance to show off their skills - Alistair puts on a masterful vocal performance with the help of an illusory back up choir, Ducky shows off his athletic prowess by outracing the two centaurs, and Grookius puts on a frankly amazing show of close up magic, which is all the more impressive considering he is unable to lie while doing so. ^summary
