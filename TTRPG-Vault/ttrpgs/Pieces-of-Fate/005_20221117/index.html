    # Session Summary

    005_20221117 The PCs make their way to Alistair’s childhood home, the city of Orphea, in the Astral Sea. On the way they meet a grifter named Captain Kris Kraft. After setting him on his way, they arrive and make their way to the Theodellus family home. Although they hoped to make a quick and quiet entrance and exit, they are quickly noticed by the household staff and spend the evening feasting and speaking with Alistair Theodellus I, Alistair’s father. ^summary

    # Log

    The PCs left Illis and set out toward Orphea

    • On the way

      • While on route, a Damselfly ship flying Guild colors was spotted off the starboard side, approaching the Diversity
      • The PCs opted to let it approach
      • Piloting the vessel was a grey bearded Hadozee named Captain Kris Kraft
      • Over the course of a conversation with him, it became clear that he was scamming other spelljamers into paying fake fines.
      • Alistair layed it on thick in their conversation to let him know that the PCs were academy trained and had connections to upper-level administration, so he “let them off with a warning”
      • As he left the PCs just barely saw the Guild colors be taken down and replaced with some other flag

    • the PCs arrive at Orphea without any other incidents

    • The spelljamming docks and the city are separated by a large wooded green space

    • As the PCs are making their way through en route to Alistair’s family home, they notice an abnormal number of owls, perched and watching them.

    • After interacting with one, they decide to keep moving, but notice that they are being followed.

    • Keeping ahead of the owls, the PCs eventually make it into the edge of town, turning back to notice an owl bear watching them from the woods, with some of the owls perched on it.

    • The owls all follow the owl bear back into the woods

    • The PCs make their way to the Theodellus home

    • Staff at the Theodellus household

      • Jarval the butler
      • Gertrude the maid

    • Alistair tries to get in and out of the house quickly, but Jarval is quick to tell Alistair’s father, Alistair Senior, that the PCs] are there

    • The astral elves are currently celebrating a Gehlira related holiday

    • Alistair Senior insists that they have a celebratory feast for his son’s return

    • While freshening up, Alistair Senior tells the PCs a few things about Alistair’s childhood

      • Alistair’s first time riding a horse, his cloak got caught on a thornbush, and tore itself apart on his neck, leaving a bad mark for a week
      • Alistair had a childhood friend/bully by the name of Primaltus. He was a boy who lived down the street

    • Alistair goes for a walk about the house, looking for signs that his nightmare about his home may be coming true, but only finds a childhood toy, a pull string gyroscope

    • As the PCs are about to begin feasting they see a group of figures in deep hooded blue robes painting runes on the church of Gehlira.

    • Alistair Senior seems unbothered and insists that they not worry about it and instead feast

    • Alistair Senior also mentions a “slight resurgence” in the Cult of Khot

    • The PCs and Alistair Senior retire at the end of the feast, each to their own rooms

    • The current date is 237
